Lo Bak Gou

Made of shredded radish and plain rice flour.

Cut into square slices and sometimes pan-fried before serving. Each pan-fried radish cake has a thin crunchy layer on the outside from frying and is soft on the inside.

Kuih Lapis

Serve with roasted peanut or grated coconut flesh fillings.
